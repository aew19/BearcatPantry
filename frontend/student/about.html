Meet the Team!

It began with a Student Government initiative, and then very quickly became a working product! You may not have known this, but this application was created as a senior design project. This project would not have come to fruition if it wasn't for the support of our administrators, advisors, and most importantly, the individuals from IT@UC! See below for more information on how the Stocker Application came to be.

Senior Design Students

These three students lead the planning, design, development, and implementation of the Bearcats Pantry Stocker Application. Although there were many options for senior design projects, this group wanted to contribute to something that would serve students for many years to come. This project instantly stuck out as one that would impact students from every corner of the university. Development of this project began in August 2020, and concluded in April 2021. Overall, the group contributed hundreds of hours into this project over the span of 9 months.

Technical Advisor - Dr. Nan Niu

Throughout the developement of this program, Dr. Nan Niu has served as the technical advisor of the senior design group. His many years of experience was crucial in helping us develop a program that was sustainable for many years to come.

Project Inventor - Neha Chawla

Neha Chawla is a Computer Science student, also pursuing a masters in Business Administration and a minor in Women's Gender and Sexuality Studies. Back in 2020, Neha began her campaign for Senator at Large in Undergraduate Student Government. One of the platform points from her campaign was the Bearcats Pantry Stocker Application. Once elected, she quickly worked with the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science to have this initiative finalized as a senior design project. After the project was assigned to the group above, Neha continued to serve as a project advisor until it's completion in 2021.

Bearcats Pantry Partner - Daniel Cummins

Daniel Cummins currently serves as the Assistant Dean of Students, and founded the Bearcats Pantry in 2016. Daniel's partnership in this project has been crucial in ensuring that this application will serve a wide range of students. His continued collaboration and support for the Bearcats Pantry Stocker Application will forever be appreciated!
